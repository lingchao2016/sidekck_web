What is & Why SideKcK?

SideKcK is a mobile app designed to help you easily find the Gym Buddy you need whose schedule and goal matches yours.
We believe the power of teamworks in gym, and we want to make this as easy and as fun as possible for you.

Post & find before workout
The new & easy way to buddy up

Just post your invitation with workout time, location and goal before hitting the gym.
SideKcK will spread your invitation among people and friends nearby, so that those who are interested will be able to spot and join you.
